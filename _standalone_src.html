Layout & Effects

Aurora Background

a.k.a. Gradient Mesh · Ambient Glow · Animated Gradient

A softly blurred, slowly shifting field of overlapping color gradients used as ambient page or hero backdrop — evoking the northern lights behind your content.

When to use

  • Hero sections & empty states that need depth without imagery
  • Setting brand mood behind large, sparse type
  • Login, splash & marketing surfaces

When not to use

  • Behind dense text — contrast suffers
  • Perf-sensitive or low-power devices
  • Data-dense dashboards & tables
